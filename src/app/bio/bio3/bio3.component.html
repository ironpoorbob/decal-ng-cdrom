Guitarist Bob Conlon, who came from San Francisco's melodic slop rock band, The Clavens, formed with local drummer, Chris Stark, to create the beginnings of Decal.

Originally joining S.F.'s music scene as a three piece, like all bands, they had their share of personnel problems. But, after several lineup changes, bass player Steve Faine and guitarist Chris Carroll were recruited to complete the group.



At this time the band
was called G.U.S.
